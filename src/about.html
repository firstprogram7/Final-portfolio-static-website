Chapter 04
The Website That Changed Everything

The Website That
Changed Everything.

Even while building my career at Amazon, that childhood instinct to create kept pulling at me. I started exploring web development on the side teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript first, then gradually moving into Node.js, MongoDB, React, and backend development. Not because someone told me to, but because I genuinely couldn't stop.

My first real project wasn't for a client. It was for a friend. She was an incredibly talented painter — one of those people whose work stops you mid-scroll — but nobody knew she existed online. She had no website, no digital presence, no way for the world to find her.

So I built her one. I listed every piece of her art, designed the experience around her creative identity, and put her work in front of the world. She started getting real, paying clients. She'd call me just to say thank you.

That moment told me everything I needed to know.
